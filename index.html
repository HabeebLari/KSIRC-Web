About KSIRC

The Kuwait Subiya International Racing Club & Circuit represents the pinnacle of motorsports excellence in the Middle East. Located in the heart of the Silk City development, KSIRC is Kuwait's gateway to the global Formula One community.

Our Mission

To establish Kuwait as the premier motorsports destination in the Middle East through world-class racing facilities, innovative automotive technology development, and sustainable sports tourism, while fostering the next generation of racing talent.

Our Vision

To be the Middle East's leading Formula One destination, where speed meets heritage, creating unforgettable experiences that celebrate Kuwait's rich automotive culture while setting new standards in motorsports excellence.

World-Class Facilities

Hover over marked areas for facility details.

Interactive Facilities Master Plan: Hover for Details

Main Circuit

5.85km FIA Grade 1 certified track with 18 challenging turns, designed by Hermann Tilke for world-class racing experiences.

Racing Academy

Professional driver development programs from karting to Formula racing, nurturing the next generation of Kuwaiti racing talent.

32 Pit Garages

State-of-the-art pit complex with professional-grade facilities for teams, equipped with latest technology and safety systems.

VIP Hospitality

Luxurious hospitality suites offering premium racing experiences with world-class amenities and services.

Motorsports Museum

Interactive museum showcasing Kuwait's automotive heritage and the evolution of Formula One racing technology.

Karting Circuit

Professional karting facility for beginners and competitive racing, providing the perfect entry point into motorsports.

Safety Systems

Advanced TecPro barriers, medical center, fire suppression systems, and emergency response capabilities exceeding FIA standards.

Our Racing Program

AUM - The Future of Kuwaiti Motorsports

KSIRC is proud to partner with the American University of the Middle East (AUM) and Red Bull, fostering next-generation engineering and driving talent. This collaboration supports the growth of motorsports through cutting-edge research and hands-on racing experience.

Program Highlights

  • Strategic Partnership with AUM & Red Bull
  • Formula Student Design & Competition
  • Advanced Aerodynamics Research
  • Driver Performance & Development Coaching
  • Access to FIA Grade 1 Circuit for Testing
  • Scholarship Opportunities
